About Me

My work sits at the intersection of human behavior, large-scale systems, and practical product decisions. I focus on how people discover, interpret, and trust information in modern search experiences, especially as AI generated answers become a primary entry point.

Over the years I have led teams across industry to design research programs that are rigorous enough to publish and concrete enough to influence roadmaps, launch decisions, and product narratives.
